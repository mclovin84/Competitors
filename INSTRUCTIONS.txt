Master Content Prompt — Analyze Competitors + Out-Cover Them

How to use:


Save 2–4 competitor sites (browser: Ctrl+S → "Webpage, Complete") into
competitors/[niche]/site-1/, site-2/, etc. — one folder per competitor.
Open that folder in Cursor.
Fill in the 5 VARIABLES at the top of the prompt.
Paste the whole prompt into Claude Code.


Runs in two phases: it analyzes and shows you a plan first, you approve, then it writes.
Works for ANY niche and ANY city — you only change the 5 variables.
