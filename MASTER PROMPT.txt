Step D — Content plan for the new site:
Cover EVERYTHING the competitors cover, PLUS the gaps, at 2–3x their depth:
- Full page list (services, hub pages, blog posts)

- Full FAQ list (more questions than any single competitor)

Write all content to the approved plan, following these rules:

- Depth = broader coverage + more specific, useful detail — never filler or repetition

Originality (critical):
- Original wording throughout. Never copy competitor sentences or phrasing.

Localization:
- Make everything specific to [CITY] using the Step A local research.
- Never include the "NOT true of this city" items from Step A.

Intent:

SEO (every page):
