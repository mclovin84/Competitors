Water Damage Experts in Hill County, TX, and Surrounding Areas

SCM Cleaning & Restoration, located in Hill County, TX, has been a family-owned and operated business since 1969. We specialize in water damage restoration and are committed to providing reliable, honest services with fast response times and free estimates. Our experienced team understands the stress and disruption that water damage can cause, and we work quickly to minimize the impact on your home or business.


With decades of experience, we have honed our techniques to ensure the most efficient and effective restoration process. We handle everything from initial water extraction to thorough drying and dehumidification, mold prevention, and complete restoration of affected areas. Our goal is to restore your property to its pre-damage condition as swiftly as possible while ensuring the highest quality of service.

Residential and Commercial Water Damage Restoration in Hill County, San Antonio, and Surrounding Areas of Texas

We provide water damage restoration services for both residential and commercial properties. Our experienced team quickly assesses the damage, removes water, dries affected areas, and restores your property to its original condition. We serve Hill County, San Antonio, and surrounding areas of Texas, ensuring a thorough and efficient restoration process. Water damage can cause a variety of issues in your home or business, including:

  • Clogged Sewer Lines
  • Leaking or Burst Water Heaters
  • Roof or Window Leaks
  • Burst Pipes
  • Blocked Bathtub Drains
  • Pet-Related Moisture Damage
  • Plumbing Issues
  • Poor Washer or Dishwasher Installation
  • Accidental Damage to Pipes

Dealing with water damage is stressful enough without having to navigate insurance claims. We help you through the process by providing detailed documentation and photos of the damage to support your insurance claims.
